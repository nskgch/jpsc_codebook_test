Number of surveys¶

Variable name: PANEL¶

Description¶

The variable indicates the total number of the surveys conducted, beginning with the first survey in 1993.

New sample members were added in each of the fifth, eleventh, sixteenth, and twenty-first surveys. The data from these surveys are separated into two types (those for the existing sample members and those for the newly added sample members: e.g., 5a and ab for the fifth survey and 11ab and 11c for the eleventh survey ). The value of PANEL, however, is the same for both data types.
